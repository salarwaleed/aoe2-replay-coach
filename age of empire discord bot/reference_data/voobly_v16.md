# Voobly v1.6 Server Reference

## Starting Conditions
- **Age:** Imperial Age from second zero — no Dark Age, Feudal, or Castle progression
- **Resources:** ~1000 Food, ~750 Wood, ~300 Stone, ~500 Gold (confirmed from live scoreboards)
- Standard AoE2 unit and tech rules apply; no known unit/tech restrictions beyond vanilla

## Key Building Costs (relevant to this ruleset)
- Town Center: 275 Wood + 100 Stone
- Farm: 60 Wood
- House: 30 Wood
- Barracks: 175 Wood
- Stable: 175 Wood
- Castle: 650 Stone

## Pocket Boom Opening (math from confirmed starting resources)

**Goal:** 3 TCs running constant villager production, farms feeding them.

1. **Immediately** drop TC #2 and TC #3 (275W + 100S each = 550W + 200S total)
   - Leaves ~200W and ~100S from starting stockpile
2. Queue villagers across all 3 TCs (50F each); 1000F = ~20 villagers queued before farming starts
3. Spend remaining ~200W on ~3 farms (60W each) to sustain food income
4. Send all existing villagers to Wood; shift to Food/Gold as farms and income grow
5. Build Barracks/Stable (175W each) once wood income is established (~60–90s in)
6. Transition to military when you have 40+ villagers and stable income across all resources

**Stone note:** 300S covers exactly 3 TCs (100S × 3 additional = wait, 2 additional TCs = 200S, leaving 100S for early wall/tower or banking for a Castle later at 650S)

## Villager Allocation Targets
- **Early (0–2 min):** 60% Wood, 30% Food (farms), 10% Gold
- **Mid (2–5 min):** 40% Food, 35% Wood, 20% Gold, 5% Stone
- **Late (5+ min):** Balance to match unit production demand; prioritize Gold + Food for army

## Team Roles
- **Pocket:** Booms aggressively — 2nd and 3rd TC immediately, maxes villager count before military
- **Flank:** Lighter eco start, faster military transition to protect the pocket and apply pressure
