# AoE2 Game Rates Reference (WK / UserPatch — base rates, no civ bonuses)

## Villager Gather Rates (per villager, per minute)
- Farm: ~18.5 food/min (diminishes slightly above ~6 vills per farm cluster)
- Wood (near Lumber Camp): ~20 wood/min
- Gold (near Mining Camp): ~17 gold/min
- Stone (near Mining Camp): ~17 stone/min
- Foraging (berries): ~18 food/min
- Hunting: ~20 food/min

Rule of thumb: sustaining 1 unit of a resource per minute ≈ 1 villager on that resource.

## Town Center Production
- 1 villager per 25 seconds | Cost: 50 Food
- 3 TCs = 1 new villager every ~8s at peak output
- With 1000F starting: ~20 villagers can be queued before farming income kicks in

## Quick Math Examples
- 2 Stables running knights: need ~6 vills on food + ~9 vills on gold to sustain indefinitely
- 1 Barracks running champions: need ~3 vills on food + ~1 vill on gold
- 3 TCs producing vills: need ~12 vills on food minimum to sustain
